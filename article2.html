Vocabulary Transcript Discussion

culmination

Definition: The final result or climax of something, often after a long process.

Example: This breakthrough is the culmination of decades of research.

decades

Definition: Periods of ten years.

Example:The research has been ongoing for several decades.

transplant

Definition:A medical operation in which an organ or tissue is moved from one person's body to another's.

Example: The future may involve organ transplants from pigs to humans.

clinical

Definition: Relating to the observation and treatment of patients.

Example: The company is preparing for clinical trials to test the new technology.

facility

Definition: A place, building, or equipment provided for a particular purpose.

Example: The new facility houses a cloning lab and a research farm.

pathogen

Definition: A bacterium, virus, or other microorganism that can cause disease.

Example: The facility is designed to keep out harmful pathogens.

decontaminated

Definition: Made safe by removing or neutralizing harmful substances or contaminants.

Example: Everything going in and out of the facility is decontaminated.

sufficient

Definition: Enough; adequate for the purpose.

Example: The FDA needs to determine if the data is sufficient to proceed with clinical trials.
